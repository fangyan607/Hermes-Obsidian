志道据德依仁游艺 - 古典线描意境场景

FOCAL POINT: 四层山水意境递进——从山下"游于艺"到山顶"志于道"，如登山修行

ATMOSPHERE: 山峦叠嶂，云雾缭绕，层层递进，意境高远

ZONES:
- Zone 1 (底层-山下): "游于艺"——一位雅士抚琴，身旁有棋盘、书画，自由畅游于技艺中，如鱼在水中
- Zone 2 (中层-山腰): "依于仁"——行者以仁心待人，途中扶助老者，行为准则
- Zone 3 (上层-山巅前): "据于德"——修者坚定立于磐石之上，以德为根基，立身之本
- Zone 4 (顶层-云端): "志于道"——一人在最高处仰望天道，方向目标，墨线渐淡化为云气

LABELS: "志于道" "据于德" "依于仁" "游于艺"

COLORS: Xuan Paper background (#F5E6D3), Ink Black (#2A2A2A) for all line work, Cinnabar Red (#C23B22) sparingly for seal stamp and the peak of "志于道", Ink wash gradients from dark (#4A4A4A) at base to pale (#D8D8D8) at peak, Pale Ink (#E8E8E8) for clouds at summit

STYLE: Traditional Chinese bai-miao (白描) line drawing style. Vertical layered mountain composition (山水立轴 format). Each layer represents one level of Confucian cultivation, ascending from bottom (art/skills) to top (the Way). Ink wash becomes progressively lighter toward the summit, symbolizing the ethereal nature of the highest pursuit. Small figures at each layer engaged in corresponding activities. Traditional Chinese mountain painting conventions (皴法 for rock textures). Generous white space in the clouds between layers. Clean composition with generous white space. Vertical flow from bottom to top.

ASPECT: 2:3 (vertical scroll format)

Human figures: Classical Chinese line drawing style (白描), small but expressive figures at each mountain layer, each in pose matching their cultivation level — playing music at base, helping others mid-way, standing firm above, gazing upward at peak, not photorealistic.

Text should be large and prominent with handwritten-style Chinese calligraphy fonts. Keep minimal, focus on the four key Confucian phrases.
