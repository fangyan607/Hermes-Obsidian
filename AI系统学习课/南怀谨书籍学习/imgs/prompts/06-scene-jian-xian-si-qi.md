见贤思齐 - 古典线描意境场景

FOCAL POINT: 一位学子居于中间，左观贤者（正面镜），右看不贤者（反面镜），自我升华

ATMOSPHERE: 如镜自照，明暗对照，内省而精进

ZONES:
- Zone 1 (左侧): 一位贤者身披儒袍，手持书卷，气度从容，象征"正面镜子"——照出自己不足
- Zone 2 (中心): 学子站立中间，面朝前方，双目微闭内省，左右各有一面古铜镜
- Zone 3 (右侧): 一个不贤者举止轻浮，象征"反面镜子"——检查自己是否有同样问题
- Zone 4 (上方): 学子头顶浮现向上的箭头或阶梯，象征"自我提升"

LABELS: "见贤思齐焉" "见不贤而内自省也" "正面镜" "反面镜"

COLORS: Xuan Paper background (#F5E6D3), Ink Black (#2A2A2A) for all line work, Cinnabar Red (#C23B22) sparingly for the mirrors' reflections and seal stamp, Pale Ink Wash (#C8C8C8) for background

STYLE: Traditional Chinese bai-miao (白描) line drawing style. Tripartite composition with the learner at center, virtuous person on left, unwise person on right. The two "mirrors" rendered as traditional Chinese bronze mirrors (铜镜) with reflective surfaces. Classical Han dynasty attire. Linear flow from left (inspiration) through center (reflection) to right (caution) and upward (improvement). Generous white space between the three figures. Clean composition with generous white space.
